TDV Partners: Our Lead Investor on Why They Backed QuantE Energy

View the LinkedIn Feature

TDV Partners announce their decision to lead our seed round, to support energy independence and India's transition to clean power.

For India’s ambitious target of 500 GW of renewable capacity by 2030, distributed solar will play a crucial role. In a country where almost all residential rooftop potential remains untapped. We are seamlessly removing the blockers of high upfront costs, poor servicing, limited awareness, and inconsistent project quality.

As TDV put it, our founders,Akshat Khare and Ankush Vashisht are building QuantE to democratize access to clean, affordable, and smart energy for the residential sector with a pinpointed focus on housing societies.

We're elated to have TDV Partners leading the round, alongside prominent investors including Raghunandan G (Zolve, TaxiForSure), Amit Lakhotia (Park+), Hari Krishnan Nair (Great Learning), and Ankit Gupta (Brookfield Properties).

We're grateful for their belief — and ready to build.
