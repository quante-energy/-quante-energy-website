QuantE Featured in Construction World: Zero-Capex Solar for India's Housing Societies

📰 Featured on Construction World.in

QuantE Energy Tech is proud to be featured in Construction World, one of India's leading infrastructure and real estate publications. This recognition highlights our commitment to making clean energy accessible through innovative solar solutions for residential housing societies.

In the feature, our co-founders, Akshat Khare and Ankush Vashisht, explain how QuantE's Zero-Capex Solar model enables residential societies to reduce electricity costs by up to 50% without any upfront investment.

The article also highlights how QuantE is transforming rooftop solar adoption through a service-led approach supported by AI and IoT-enabled monitoring, ensuring transparency, reliability, and hassle-free operations for every housing society.

We are honored to be recognized by Construction World and remain committed to accelerating India's transition toward a cleaner, smarter, and more sustainable energy future.
