Press Release: QuantE Energy Raises $500K Seed to Bring Zero-Cost Solar to India's Housing Societies

QuantE Energy raised $500,000 in seed funding, led by Trillion Dollar Venture (TDV) Partners, with participation from a remarkable group of angel investors — including

Raghunandan G (founder of Zolve and TaxiForSure), Amit Lakhotia (founder of Park+), Ankit Gupta, and Hari Krishnan Nair (co-founder of Great Learning).

Their belief in our idea and work means a great deal to us.

This funding accelerates our early pilots across housing societies in Mumbai, Pune, and Delhi-NCR.

We believe India's path to its 2030 renewable goal happens on its rooftops.

Thank you to our investors, partners, and early adopters for believing in this journey.
